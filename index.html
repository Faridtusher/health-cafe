BMI Calculator

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight to estimate body fat. It's widely used to categorize individuals into underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese, based on a numerical scale. While BMI is a useful screening tool, it doesn't directly measure body fat or account for muscle mass, so it may not fully reflect an individual's health.
